FueVolt was created to help Australian drivers save money on fuel and make the transition to electric vehicles easier. Frequently Asked Questions

Where does FueVolt get its fuel prices?
FueVolt pulls real-time fuel prices directly from official Australian government sources. Prices are updated throughout the day as fuel stations report changes.
Is FueVolt free to use?
Yes, FueVolt is completely free. There are no subscriptions, premium tiers, or hidden fees.
What connector types can I filter by?
FueVolt supports filtering by Type 2, CCS2, CHAdeMO, and Tesla connectors. You can also filter by charging speed: slow (up to 7kW), fast (7-50kW), and ultra-rapid (50kW+).
Does FueVolt track my location?
FueVolt only accesses your location if you grant permission, and it is used solely to find nearby stations. Your location data is never stored, sold, or shared.
