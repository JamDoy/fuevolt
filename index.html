Fuel Prices

Compare real-time petrol, diesel, E10, U95, U98 and LPG prices from official Australian government APIs. FueVolt pulls live pricing data from the NSW Motor API, Victoria Fair Fuel Open Data API (Servo Saver), Queensland Fuel Pricing Direct API, and Western Australia FuelWatch. Search by suburb or postcode to find the cheapest fuel station near you. Results sorted by price, drive time, or distance powered by TomTom routing. Covers over 8,000 fuel stations across New South Wales, Victoria, Queensland and Western Australia with prices updated in real-time throughout the day.

EV Charging Stations

Locate thousands of electric vehicle charging points across Australia with real-time charger availability. Filter stations by connector type including Type 2, CCS2, CHAdeMO and Tesla. Choose between slow charging (up to 7kW), fast charging (7-50kW) and ultra-rapid charging (50kW+). Real-time availability shows green for available chargers, red for in-use, and grey for out-of-service stations. EV data sourced from Open Charge Map with availability from the TomTom EV Charging Stations Availability API.

Trip Planner

Plan Australian road trips with optimal fuel stops and EV chargers along your route. Enter start and end destinations to get the best route with real-time traffic conditions, accurate drive time estimates, fuel cost calculations, and EV battery level forecasts at each waypoint. The EV route planner calculates battery consumption, suggests optimal charging stops, and shows estimated charge levels. Powered by TomTom Routing API and Extended Routing API for electric vehicles.
