EV Charging Stations in Perth

Perth's EV charging network is expanding across the metro area, with chargers from Joondalup to Rockingham. Western Australia's vast distances make the growing network of fast chargers along major highways particularly important for EV drivers.

Connector Types in Perth

  • Type 2 — most common AC connector in Australia, used for destination and home charging (up to 22kW)
  • CCS2 (Combined Charging System) — the standard for DC fast charging (50kW to 350kW)
  • CHAdeMO — older DC fast charging standard, used by Nissan Leaf and some Mitsubishi models
  • Tesla — Tesla's proprietary connector, available at Tesla Supercharger and Destination locations

Charging Speeds

Filter Perth chargers by speed: Slow (up to 7kW, ideal for overnight charging), Fast (7-50kW, adds range in 1-2 hours), or Ultra-Rapid (50kW+, 80% charge in 20-40 minutes).
